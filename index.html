The Platform

Two layers of intelligence. One seamless night.

Dreamz is a wearable sleep technology device that uses non-invasive brain sensing and gentle electrical stimulation to improve sleep quality. It targets deep sleep, sleep onset, and REM sleep stages, helping people with insomnia, poor sleep quality, and racing thoughts fall asleep faster and wake up more refreshed. The device pairs with a mobile app for sleep tracking, sleep scores, and personalised insights.

01

Brain Activity Sensing

While you sleep, Dreamz quietly listens to your brain's electrical rhythms - detecting which stage of sleep you're in and spotting the moments when you need a gentle nudge deeper.

02

Gentle Brain Stimulation

At precisely the right moments, Dreamz delivers a barely-perceptible electrical signal to support your brain's natural transition into deeper, more restorative sleep.

03

Gets Smarter Every Night

Dreamz learns what works for your unique brain over time. The more you wear it, the more precisely it's tuned to you - progressively deepening its impact on your sleep.

04

Sleep Insights App

Wake up to a clear sleep score and nightly brain activity summary. Track trends over weeks and months, and choose outcomes - from deeper sleep to enhanced memory and focus.

05

Always Listening, Always Responding

Unlike simple timers or fixed programs, Dreamz responds to what your brain is actually doing in the moment - never stimulating when it isn't needed, always acting when it counts.

06

Overnight Comfort

Engineered for 8+ hours of wear - breathable fabric, rear weight distribution, and a slim 3D-printed electronics module that disappears once you close your eyes.
